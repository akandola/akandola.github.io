About

The longer version
Work

My path has zig-zagged through neuroscience research, software engineering, and product management — and along the way I’ve picked up the toolkit to step into almost any industry and tackle its hardest problems. What I really care about is the messy, important work: figuring out what’s broken, why it matters, and shipping something that actually moves the needle. I’ve built CRM integrations for real-estate companies, benefit delivery platforms for senior citizens, and wage-theft reclamation tools for asylum seekers and legal aid advocates.

Civic

Outside of work, I get involved in local and grassroots movements — volunteering on city council campaigns, collecting census data, and participating in public hearings for legislation affecting my friends and family.

Joy

For fun, I curate playlists and chase new music, watch F1, play basketball, and choreograph and dance bhangra. I’m always hunting for the best dairy-free pizza in any city I land in, listening to history and tech podcasts, and making memento boxes — keepsakes, photo books, and odds and ends to capture each year.
